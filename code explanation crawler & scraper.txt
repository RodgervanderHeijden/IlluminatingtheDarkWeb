The code crawls and scrapes information of the dark web market Torrez.
First all nessecary packages are imported. Then connection to TOR and the Torrez market is realised. 
Subsequently the crawler is written that collects all URLs of the pages and goes through them all of 
them by a for loop. For all pages the relevant information is scraped using the package BeatifulSoup. 
Finally, all information is combined and coverted to tabular and CSV format ready for analysis.
